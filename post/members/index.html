Da Chen

Ph. D., Associate professor, master supervisor, graduated from the Paris University of Arts and Sciences in 2017. From October 2016 to March 2019, he worked as a postdoctoral fellow at dauphine University and the National Eye Hospital of Paris. His research interests include image analysis based on deep learning, medical image processing, deformation modeling and computational geometry based on variational methods and partial differential equations. Member of Chinese Society of Stereology. In recent years, he has hosted the National Youth Fund and published more than 20 papers as the first author or corresponding author in the top academic journals and conferences in the field of computer vision and applied mathematics.
